  Narad (Richard Eggenberger)


Richard Eggenberger was given the name 'Narad' by the Mother in 1972. Her first Divine Command to him was to bring down a New Music for which he formed the OM Choirs now in many countries. Presently he is concentrating on producing videos on many subjects. The first series is, 'Sri Aurobindo Ashram, the Early Years' with interviews of disciples who grew up in the Ashram under the Mother's guidance and some who had darshan of Sri Aurobindo. The second series, 'Interviews with Disciples and Devotees' includes many accepted by the Mother. More recent series include poetry readings 'Sri Aurobindo's Poetry' and poets whom Sri Aurobindo noted were bringing down poetry from the higher planes. It is titled, 'Seeking the New Poetry'. A series on inspired modern poets has begun and a current series on Sri Aurobindo's magnum opus, Savitri, 'titled 'Explorations in Savitri' with Dr. Alok Pandey, studies in depth this greatest of all epic poems.
